Welcome to my site!

This is still under construction, but feel free to wander around.

My name is Sinan (or "Hugged" from osu!). Alottakeys is my personal project to build a central platform for osu!mania's 10K+ community. The idea started with data archival and visualization as I wanted to build stat profiles for players and beatmaps (which is all there is so far), but I have much more planned.
